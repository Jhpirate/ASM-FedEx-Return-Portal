What Do I DO?

Although most of the portal is self explanatory, some fields do need to be explained.

For most of the portal you should just go through and fill in all the required fields with your store’s information. If you do not feel comfortable putting in you or your managers name, simply enter in “MANAGER” in the First Name field and “STORE #” followed by your store number in the Last Name field.

The most important field you will need to fill out is labeled "Full Store Number" and is located under the "Merchant Supplied Additional Information" section. In this field you MUST enter in your assigned store number AND prefix (Ex. CK4701234). Incorrect information may resulted in a delay in recieving your credit.
