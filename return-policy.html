Return Policy

Before returning any items, please read through the information below:  

General Policy:

  1. All items returned must be in sellable condition.
  2. Items should be returned in a reasonably sized box. Larger boxes with more products have a higher probability of arriving damaged.
    • If your store needs more than one return label, please fill out the return portal more than once.
  3. Returns should be packed with adequate packing material to ensure the product does not shift around and therefore get damaged in its journey to our return facility.
    • Any damaged products due to improper packaging will be billed to your store.
  4. All like items should be bundled together to minimize the risk of damage during transit.
    • (Ex. Pokémon cards should be bundled together with rubber bands to prevent individual card packs from sliding around the box.)
  5. All product returned should be free of price tags.
    • Any price tag placed on product needs to be removed prior to sending back your return.
  6. Any items returned should be free from stains.
  7. Ensure any items returned are items that were sent by All Sports Marketing. Any items that do not belong to us will not be sent back unless your store is willing to pay for return shipping.
    • If we receive items that do not belong to us, your store will be notified by someone from All Sports.
    • Items that do not belong to us will be held for 90 days. If we do not hear from you by then, we will assume you no longer want the items and they will become property of All Sports Marketing, Inc.

Item Specific Policies:

  1. TeenyMates Big Sip Cups & Mugs:
    • When sending these items back, they all NEED to be individually bagged in their original plastic bags or in individual grocery bags. Without protection, these items WILL arrive damaged and scuffed, no matter how neatly they are packed, rendering them unsellable.
  2. Pokémon / Fortnite / Panini Donruss / Other Trading Cards:
    • All cards need to arrive bundled together by release in a new, unopened state. Please try not to exceed 12 cards per bundle to prevent warping/bending of the packs.
    • (Ex. Pokémon Sword & Shield—Rebel Clash should be bundled separate from Pokémon Sword & Shield—Darkness Ablaze)
  3. Funko Boxes:
    • Funkos should be packed in the separate boxes they arrived in or in a way to ensure they will not be crushed, torn, or otherwise damaged during transit.
    • Crushed, torn, or otherwise damaged Funkos due to improper packing/packaging will be billed to your store.
  4. Squeezy Mates:
    • All Squeezy Mates should be placed together in a separate bag to prevent scuffing and tearing.
  5. Keychains & Other Novelty Items:
    • All items must arrive in the condition they were sent. This includes ensuring items have all their original packaging material as well as hanging tags.
