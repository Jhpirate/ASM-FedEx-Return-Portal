What Do I Do?

Although most of the portal is self-explanatory, some fields do need a little bit of explanation.

In the 'First Name' field enter in your retailer name (Ex. "Circle K"). In the 'Last Name' field, enter in your store number (Ex. "#123456"). All other information can be completed with your store's information.

The most important field you will need to fill out is labeled 'Full Store Number' and is located under the 'Merchant Supplied Additional Information' section. In this field, you MUST enter your assigned store number AND prefix (Ex. CK4701234). Incorrect information may result in a delay in receiving your credit. See the table located below for your corresponding store prefix.

When shipping product back, make sure all product is carefully packed in a reasonably sized box. Any damaged products due to improper packaging will be billed to your store.
